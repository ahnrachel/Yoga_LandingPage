Classes

BEGINNERS

A progressive series made for beginners

You will learn basic postures and breathing techniques that will help to relieve stress, improve flexibility and strength, and increase your sense of well-being. You do not need to be flexible or have any previous experience with yoga in order to take this class.

FUNCTIONAL FLOW

Great class for beginners

Functional Flow classes focus on alignment, adapting poses, working with the breath, and increasing flexibility, strength, and balance. Assistance by way of props may be utilized to allow greater access, opening, and awareness throughout the body, and to allow students of various levels to practice safely and comfortably.

YIN & YANG

Balance your energy and quiet your mind.

In the first half of the class you'll move through active yoga poses, linking breath and movement to harness your energy and warm the body. Wind down during the second half of class with a sequence of Yin poses, holding each for several minutes to create a calming, meditative effect. Combining a Vinyasa Flow (yang) and a Yin practice promotes better mobility and strength in your joints, ligaments, and deep connective tissues, while also challenging your mind to stay present.

ENERGY FLOW

A connection between breath and movement

Classes may be faster or slower paced depending on the instructor. Expect music (sometimes upbeat) to inspire movement and personal power. Props may be used for support. Classes are suitable for all levels, but some yoga experience is helpful.
